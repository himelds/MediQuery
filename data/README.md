# Data Collections

## medical
MedQuAD dataset (16,163 medical QA pairs)

## clinical
Clinical reference documents:
- Drug formulary
- Treatment protocols
- Diagnostic reference

## nursing
Nursing procedures and infection control guidelines.

## billing
Insurance billing, claims, and reimbursement documentation.

## equipment
Biomedical equipment operation and maintenance manuals.

## general
Staff handbook, leave policy, code of conduct, FAQs.

## Data Sources
- **Medical Q&A:** MedQuAD dataset (16,163 documents) — [NLM/NIH](https://github.com/abachaa/MedQuAD)
- **Hospital operational documents:** Adapted from [jaymin7359/mediobot-assignment](https://github.com/jaymin7359/mediobot-assignment) (billing, equipment, nursing, general)
